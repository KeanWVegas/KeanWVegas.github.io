Project Proposal

Home

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to show interested users information on how to begin, learning how to ride BMX, what parks are nearby them, and the fun that comes with BMX.

Audience

My audience would be 12-25 year olds who have a lot of free time to spend. They are looking to begin or get better at BMX and would use my site to retrieve information on the sport.

Persona

  • Name: Jacob Smith
  • Age: 18
  • Gender: Male
  • Family Status: Single, living at home
  • Hobbies: Extreme Sports
  • Goal: Learn about BMX and how to get started

Scenario

Jacob is heavily into snowboarding and skiing, but it's currently Summer, so there's no snow to ride on. He is looking into other hobbies, from Lacrosse to Skateboarding, and has settled on learning how to ride BMX. He can use my "How to get started" page to learn where he can purchase a bike, as well as use my "Nearby Parks" page to find where to begin. He can return to the first page to be able to find resources on beginning tricks and how to actually ride the bike.

Webpages Within

  • What is BMX? - A short description of the sport and it's history
  • How to get started - A page with links to BMX shops, forums linking riders together, videos to learn from, etc.
  • Nearby Parks - Pulls from google maps to show them where their nearest skatepark is.
  • Famous Riders - A page for showcasing the most famous riders of all time and what they did for the sport.
  • My History in BMX - Explains my relation to BMX, how I got started, why I enjoy the sport etc.

Style

I plan on having a very symplistic design that is very easy to navigate. It will allow the user to obtain as much information as possible without having to work to get it.

I will keep a clean, easy UI with the simple colors light gray and ivory. the Header/NavBar/content areas will make use of the ivory, while the gray is used behind the containers.

The font I plan on using for my Headers is called 'Dosis' on google fonts. The font I plan on using for my Content is called 'Yanone Kaffeesatz' on google fonts.

JSON

My JSON pages include my "Famous Riders" and "My History" pages.
